## Response style

- Do not narrate your plan before acting. Just execute.
- No preamble, no "I'll now..." or "Let me..." framing.
- Skip summaries of what you did unless something failed or needs a decision from me.
- If a task is unambiguous, do it — don't ask for confirmation on routine steps.
- Keep any necessary explanation to the end, and only if non-obvious.

## Tool usage

- Be conservative with background agents. You should not use them for simple routine
  tasks. I.e. don't spawn agents for routine codebase searches. Search directly with
  grep/glob/read. Use agents only when a task genuinely needs a broad multi-location
  sweep or parallel independent workstreams.
